Every watch screen, cinematic

Four screens. Every one tuned for the wrist.

The NaviBeat Watch app ships alongside the iPhone app — pair your Watch and it installs automatically. From the wrist you get a focused Now Playing with edge-to-edge cover art, a Downloads list of everything pinned offline, and Artists / Albums browsing pulled from your server whenever it's reachable. Four screens, zero fluff.

01 / Now Playing

Edge-to-edge album art on the wrist.

The Now Playing screen uses every pixel the watch face has: cover art fills the top, title and artist sit on a single row, and the transport buttons stretch across the bottom — big enough to hit mid-run, with haptics on every tap. The Digital Crown scrubs the timeline. No mini-player chrome, no ornaments, no "Watch-style" compromise.

  • Full-bleed cover art, auto-scaled to the watch screen size
  • Large-tap transport (previous / pause / next)
  • Digital Crown scrubs the progress timeline
  • Haptic feedback on every button press

02 / Downloads

Everything you've pinned, ready offline.

The Downloads tab lists every track you've pinned for offline playback from the iPhone app. Leave the phone at home, leave the Wi-Fi at home, leave even the cellular watch plan at home — the music stays on the wrist. Tracks are stored locally on the watch; tap one to start playing over Bluetooth headphones. No buffering, no network round-trips, no drop-outs on a trail run.

  • Albums / playlists / artists pinned on iPhone auto-sync here
  • Plays through Bluetooth headphones without the phone
  • LRU cache managed locally — oldest items evict first
  • Works with Watch cellular and Wi-Fi-only models alike

03 / Albums

Your full library, surfaced row by row.

The Albums tab shows every album on your Navidrome server as a scrollable list, each row with a thumbnail, album title and artist. The Digital Crown scrolls. Tap to drill into the track list. Works whenever the Watch can reach your server — LAN, cellular, Wi-Fi hotspot — or fall back to Downloads when the signal drops.

  • Scrollable list of every album on the server
  • Thumbnail + title + artist, tuned for the watch canvas
  • Digital Crown scrolls through the list naturally
  • Graceful fallback to pinned Downloads when offline

04 / Artists

Artists, with live track counts.

The Artists tab lists every artist on your Navidrome server with a live track count — so you can see at a glance which artists actually have music on the server vs. which are metadata stubs. Tap through for the artist's discography. Three-dot menu on each row for Play All, Shuffle, and jump-to-album actions, all thumb-friendly.

  • Live track counts per artist from the server
  • Three-dot row menu: Play All, Shuffle, jump-to-album
  • Microphone accent icon matches the NaviBeat design language
  • Search-by-first-letter via the Digital Crown

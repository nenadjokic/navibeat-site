The Watch app syncs pinned albums over Wi-Fi while charging. On a run, the tracks play directly from the Watch over Bluetooth headphones. No phone required, no streaming, no interruptions.

01 / Offline playback

Pin albums. Run without your phone.

Sync albums or playlists to your Watch over Wi-Fi while it charges. Leave your phone at home. The music plays from local storage directly to Bluetooth headphones.

Run free.

02 / Digital Crown volume

Spin to adjust. No tapping required.

The Digital Crown controls volume during playback. No accidental taps on a sweaty screen — just turn the crown. Skip tracks with the side button during workouts.

03 / Album & artist browsing

Your full library on a tiny screen.

Browse Albums and Artists directly from the Watch. Tap into any album for the track list. Tap any track to start playback. The full library in your pocket — and on your wrist.

04 / Automatic sync

Pinned albums sync while you sleep.

Download is triggered automatically when your Watch is on the charger and connected to Wi-Fi. Wake up, unplug, go — the music is there. No manual sync button needed.

Every screen

A music app that feels at home on iPhone.

Tab bar navigation, full-screen Now Playing, time-synced lyrics that scroll in sync with the music, and an Up Next queue you can reorder by drag-and-drop. The controls you expect, where you expect them.

01 / Now Playing

Full-screen album art, every control visible.

The Now Playing view fills the screen with album art and transport controls at thumb level. Swipe up for the Up Next queue. Swipe down to minimize. Everything reachable with one hand.

02 / Time-Synced Lyrics

Every word, exactly when it lands.

The lyrics view scrolls automatically in sync with the music. The active line highlights in amber. Tap any line to jump to that position in the track. Works with .lrc files and server-side lyrics.

03 / Home & Library

Tab bar, familiar structure, fast browsing.

Home, Artists, Albums, Playlists, Radio — all accessible from the tab bar. The standard iOS music-app layout you already know, connected to your Navidrome server instead of a streaming service.

04 / Up Next Queue

Drag, reorder, skip. Queue stays in sync.

Swipe up from Now Playing to see the Up Next queue. Drag tracks to reorder. The queue persists across app switches and lock-screen playback. AutoMix keeps music going when the queue runs out.

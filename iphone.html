Every tab, every screen

A full Navidrome client in your pocket — not a scaled-down desktop app.

Home with Pick Up Where You Left Off, Shuffle Mix, Recently Added. A full Library with Artists, Albums, Songs, Favorite Songs, Genres, Playlists, Radios, Directories and Downloads. Full-screen Now Playing with time-synced lyrics and an Up Next queue. A long-press menu that unlocks the sleep timer, AutoMix, and Show Artist / Album jumps — all in thumb reach.

01 / Home

Pick up where you left off. Every single time.

The Home tab opens on a large Pick Up Where You Left Off card — the last album you were listening to, one tap from Play. Below: a Quick Play row (Shuffle Mix, Random Album), your Most Played shelf, and a Recently Added stream. The mini player stays pinned above the tab bar so playback is always one glance away.

02 / Library

Every browse mode Navidrome exposes, in one list.

Artists. Albums. Recently Added. Recently Played. Songs. Favorite Songs. Genres. Playlists. Radios. Directories. Downloads. The Library tab mirrors the Navidrome web UI one-for-one — no features hidden in menus, no "Pro" tier gating folder browsing. Every row has a disclosure indicator and its own themed accent icon.

03 / Now Playing

Full-screen album art. Hi-Res badges. One-thumb transport.

The Now Playing view fills the entire screen with cover art. Track title, artist and album underneath, Hi-Res and bit-depth / sample-rate badges rendered directly against the artwork. Scrub bar with live elapsed / remaining, shuffle, prev, play/pause, next, repeat, and a second row for AirPlay, queue, favorite, lyrics and more — all tuned for thumb reach on large iPhones.

04 / Up Next

A queue you can actually see, with AutoPlay at the top.

Tap the queue icon and a translucent sheet slides up over Now Playing. AutoPlay toggle pinned at the top. Scrollable list of upcoming tracks, each with a thumbnail, title, artist, duration and a drag grip. Reorder with a long-press, swipe to remove, Clear All to wipe the queue without stopping playback. The server syncs the new order in the background.

05 / Time-synced lyrics

LRC lyrics that scroll with the audio.

When your Navidrome server has LRC lyrics attached, the Lyrics sheet scrolls them in sync with the music. Current line highlighted in accent orange, past lines fade out, upcoming lines wait in muted type. Supports embedded lyrics, sidecar LRC files, and the Subsonic getLyricsBySongId endpoint. Falls back to plain scrollable text when no timings exist.

06 / Long-press menu

Sleep timer, AutoMix, Show Artist — one gesture away.

Long-press anywhere on the Now Playing artwork and a stacked context menu appears. Sleep Timer with 5 / 10 / 15 / 30 / 45 minute and 1 hour presets (audio fades, not a hard cut). Show Artist / Show Album jumps straight into the detail pages. Rate submenu with stars. Favorite toggle. AutoMix on/off for endless playback. Fits in one thumb stroke from anywhere on the screen.

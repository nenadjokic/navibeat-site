Every screen

Your library, with room to explore.

The iPad layout takes advantage of the larger canvas. Persistent sidebar, split view, and full-screen lyrics side-by-side with your queue — the whole app visible at once, with no scrolling required.

01 / Home

Sidebar + Featured Album, at a glance.

Persistent sidebar with Home, Artists, Albums, Genres, Playlists, Radio, and Directories. The main area opens on a rotating Featured Album hero with Quick Play and Recently Added shelves below.

02 / Albums

Large grid, rich cover art, instant access.

The Albums view shows a larger grid than iPhone. Cover art fills the cells. Album title, artist, year, and genre visible at a glance. Tap any album for the full track list with Hi-Res badges.

03 / Lyrics + Queue

Lyrics and queue, side by side.

On iPad, the lyrics panel and Up Next queue can both be visible at once — no switching between views. Follow the lyrics while managing your queue without interrupting playback.

04 / Genres

Browse by genre, like you own the collection.

Every genre in your Navidrome library shows as a tile. Tap into any genre for all matching albums. The genre browser is the fastest way to find the right mood without knowing the exact album name.
